Skill tagging allows administrators to attach custom labels to skills, making it easier to categorize and group them. These tags provide users with an intuitive way to search and filter specific skill sets. Additionally, skill tags appear directly in the Skills Display, helping learners quickly discover, categorize, and navigate training content.

There are two options for adding skill tags:

#### Option 1: Manage from the Skill Tags Page
1. Navigate to **Project** > **Skill Tags** and click the **Tag +** button.
2. Once the tag is created, click on it to open its management page.
3. Use the skill selector dropdown at the top of the page to begin adding skills to that tag.

#### Option 2: Manage from the Skills Table
1. Navigate to the Skills page (**Project** > **Subject**).
2. Select the specific skills you want to tag.
3. Click the **Action** button located at the top-right of the skills table.
4. Select **Tag Skill** to apply your changes.
